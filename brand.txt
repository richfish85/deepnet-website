# DeepNet Solutions – Visual Brand Guidelines

---

## 1. Brand Essence
**Core Idea**: "Clarity & Confidence for Melbourne SMBs" – translating complex cybersecurity into clear, actionable protection.

**Personality**: Strategic · Reassuring · Approachable · Melbourne‑local · Forward‑thinking.

**Tone of Voice**: Plain‑English explanations, lightly technical where needed, always ending with a practical takeaway. Sprinkle in confident, conversational warmth.

---

## 2. Logo System
### 2.1 Primary Mark  
The rounded‑square icon with three flowing circuitry lines and node dots, paired with the word‑mark “DeepNet” (bold) over “SOLUTIONS” (tracking +20). Use **Deep Blue #103D6E** on light backgrounds.

### 2.2 Reverse / Mono Variants  
- **Reverse**: white icon & word‑mark on Deep Blue background.  
- **Mono**: 100 % black or white when single‑colour reproduction is required.

### 2.3 Clear‑Space  
Maintain free space equal to the height of the icon’s node circle on all sides.

### 2.4 Minimum Size  
Digital: 64 px height (logo lock‑up) · Print: 20 mm height.

### 2.5 Don’ts  
- Don’t stretch, skew, or recolour.  
- Don’t separate icon and word‑mark except for social favicons.

| Use‑case | Typeface | Weight / Style | Size Example |
|----------|----------|---------------|-------------|

| Sub‑head / H2‑H3 | Poppins | Medium | 28–40 px |
| Body | **Open Sans** | Regular | 16–18 px |
| Emphasis | Open Sans | Semibold | — |
| Code / CLI | Source Code Pro | Regular | 14 px |

> **System fallback**: “Segoe UI”, “Roboto”, “Helvetica Neue”, Arial, sans‑serif.

---

## 5. Iconography & Illustration
- **Style**: Simple line icons at 2 px stroke, rounded ends, using Primary or Accent 1.  
- **Metaphors**: Shields, locks, circuit lines, Melbourne skyline.  
- **Illustrations**: Flat, minimal; avoid overly abstract 3‑D renders.

---

## 6. Imagery Style
| Do | Don’t |
|----|-------|
| Real‑world Melbourne offices, team collaboration, night skyline with data overlay | Stock clichés of hooded hackers |
| Cool, desaturated base with Primary or Accent overlay gradients | Oversaturated colour blocking |
| Confident expressions, natural lighting | Artificial poses or dated tech imagery |

---

## 7. UI Components
- **Primary Button**:  
  - Background: Primary #103D6E  
  - Text: #FFFFFF  
  - Border‑radius: 8 px  
  - Hover: darken 6 %
- **Secondary Button**: Outline 2 px Primary, transparent fill, text Primary.
- **CTAs**: Pair button with right‑chevron icon.
- **Forms**: 12‑column grid, 24 px gutters.

---

## 8. Layout & Spacing
- **Grid**: 12‑column, 72 rem max‑width, 8 px baseline.  
- **Section Padding**: 80 px desktop · 56 px tablet · 40 px mobile.
- **Card Radius**: 16 px · Shadow: 0 4 12 px rgba(16,61,110,0.12).

---

## 9. Accessibility Guidelines
- Maintain keyboard‑navigable focus styles (#1D7ED3 outline).  
- Provide alt text describing security relevance.  
- Test colour contrast with internal checker (aim AAA for body copy if possible).

---

## 10. Example Usage
- **Hero**: Dark BG gradient (#062343→#103D6E), white H1, accent CTA.  
- **Package Cards**: Neutral 90 background, Primary headline, Accent 2 icons.  
- **Testimonials**: Light background, icon quotes in Accent 1.
